Marijuana possession, sale charges often dismissed

In 2013, 2,493 people were brought up on marijuana-related charges.

But more than half of those were dismissed by judges. The other 40%* were convicted.

Of those convicted, most faced a fine of $100.

Another 94 were discharged without punishment...

...And 17 people were put on probation or await sentencing.

In the end, only 5.8% of those arrested found themselves found themselves either behind bars or in prison.

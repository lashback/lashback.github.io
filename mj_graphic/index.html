More than half of 2013 LI marijuana cases dismissed

In 2013, 2,694 people faced marijuana-related charges in Nassau and Suffolk counties.

But 57 percent of cases were dismissed in court. 36 percent resulted in convictions.

*Almost 7 percent of those people were charged with other crimes.

Of the people convicted, most faced a fine.

Another 94 convicted were given a discharge and received no sentence.

Seventeen people were put on probation.

Another 50 are either awaiting sentencing or faced some other sentence that didn't involve jailtime.

In 2013, only 5.38 percent of all cases ended up with the defendant behind bars.

Total: 2,694 cases
